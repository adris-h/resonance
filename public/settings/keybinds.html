Keybinds

Click the button, then press the key you want to assign.

Increase key:

Controls all increase actions (tempo, volume).

Decrease key:

Controls all decrease actions (tempo, volume).

Tempo reset:

Resets the selected tempo.

Play track:

Starts playback.

Master play:

Starts playback of both tracks.

Hold + use with increase/decrease buttons

Tempo:

Selects and controls the tempo.

Volume:

Adjusts the volume.

Not customizable

Equalizer:

Selects which equalizer.

Equalizer Bands:

Selects equalizer band.
